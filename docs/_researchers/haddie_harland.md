Haddie is a PhD student at Deakin University (Geelong) and Top-Up Scholarship recipient with CSIRO's Data61 Robotics and Autonomous Systems Group, with an interest in Human-Machine Collaboration. Haddie is currently researching approaches to socially responsible, interactive and human-aligned autonomous agents through the lens of apology, including implementation utilising multi-objective reinforcement learning (MORL).
